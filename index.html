Our Services

Personal Injury Support

Medical chronologies, demand letter prep, billing & lien reductions, balance verifications, and closing statements—each with legal-tone precision. For on-demand demand letters, visit ClaimWrit.com.

Construction Administration

From pre-construction planning to subcontractor/vendor coordination, document control, permit tracking, insurance compliance, and project closeout in commercial construction and land development.

Executive-Level Support

Discreet, strategic support to C-suite—complex scheduling, travel, cross-functional reporting, expense reports, and high-stake communications. Trusted by Xerox and other enterprise clients.

Project Coordination

End-to-end coordination across legal, construction, and executive projects—milestone scheduling, cost tracking, contracts, documentation flow, and cross-team communication.

Client Communication

Professional email triage & phone correspondence to maintain client relationships—prompt, precise, and always on-brand.

Reporting & Analysis

Custom reports, status summaries, and insights to support confident, data-driven decision-making.
